# Implementation Roadmap

DeepVol BTC MOVE is the flagship structured product direction. DeepVol is also becoming a Predict-native primitive trading terminal: UP and DOWN can be direct wallet-gated primitives, RANGE remains gate-protected until dedicated validation, and BTC MOVE Receipt stays the primary composed receipt product. The roadmap now routes executable frontend state through `packages/deepvol-trading-react/`, prioritizing a real BTC MOVE volatility receipt path while adding raw primitive terminal capability without turning primitives into the MVP fee surface.

Prior RangePilot work remains important validation evidence:

- PredictManager create/deposit was validated.
- Range quote, mint, readback, and redeem were validated.
- Browser range trading scaffold was built as an engineering validation surface.
- Route B wrapper, ProtocolVault, publish, and first wrapper follow were validated on Testnet.

The prior creator-follow strategy model is not the primary product direction because public on-chain strategy parameters can be copied and used to bypass high follow fees.
